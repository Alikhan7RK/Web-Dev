Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Time: 25 minutes Serves: 24 cookies

These soft, chewy chocolate chip cookies are a timeless treat that everyone loves. Perfect with a glass of milk!

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 ¼ cups
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Unsalted butter, softened 1 cup
Granulated sugar ¾ cup
Brown sugar, packed ¾ cup
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Large eggs 2
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 2 cups

Steps

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

Step 2: In a small bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.

Step 3: In a large bowl, beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy.

Step 4: Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.

Step 5: Gradually beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips.

Step 6: Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto baking sheets. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown.

Step 7: Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Source: Allrecipes - Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Time: 40 minutes Serves: 4

A rich and comforting soup that pairs perfectly with grilled cheese sandwiches. The fresh basil adds a wonderful aroma.

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Fresh tomatoes, chopped 2 lbs
Onion, chopped 1 medium
Garlic cloves, minced 4
Vegetable broth 4 cups
Fresh basil leaves ½ cup, packed
Heavy cream ½ cup
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper To taste

Steps

Step 1: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sauté until softened.

Step 2: Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 3: Pour in vegetable broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.

Step 4: Remove from heat, add fresh basil leaves, and use an immersion blender to puree until smooth.

Step 5: Stir in heavy cream and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 6: Heat gently (without boiling) for another 5 minutes before serving.

Source: Food Network - Creamy Tomato Soup

Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken

Time: 1 hour 30 minutes Serves: 4-6

Juicy, flavorful roasted chicken with crispy skin and a bright lemon-garlic aroma. Perfect for Sunday dinner!

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Whole chicken (3-4 lbs) 1
Lemons 2
Garlic cloves, peeled 8
Fresh rosemary 4 sprigs
Olive oil 3 tablespoons
Salt 1 tablespoon
Black pepper 1 teaspoon
Butter, softened 2 tablespoons

Steps

Step 1: Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Pat the chicken dry with paper towels.

Step 2: In a small bowl, mix softened butter, 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Step 3: Gently loosen the skin over the chicken breast and rub the butter mixture under the skin.

Step 4: Quarter one lemon and place it inside the chicken cavity along with 4 garlic cloves and 2 rosemary sprigs.

Step 5: Place chicken in a roasting pan, drizzle with remaining olive oil, and surround with remaining garlic cloves and lemon slices from the second lemon.

Step 6: Roast for 1 to 1½ hours, until internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and skin is golden brown.

Step 7: Let rest for 15 minutes before carving and serving.

Source: Simply Recipes - Perfect Roast Chicken
