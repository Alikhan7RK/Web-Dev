Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 8 pancakes
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Baking powder 2 tsp
Butter 2 tbsp

Step 1: Mix all ingredients in a bowl.

Step 2: Heat a pan with butter.

Step 3: Pour batter onto the pan.

Step 4: Cook until bubbles form, then flip.

Spaghetti

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 1 lb
Tomato sauce 2 cups
Ground beef 1/2 lb
Garlic 3 cloves
Cheese 1/2 cup

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in boiling water.

Step 2: Cook beef and garlic in a pan.

Step 3: Add tomato sauce to the beef.

Step 4: Mix sauce with spaghetti and add cheese.
